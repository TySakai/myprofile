SolPOD Test Results

Over the course of five months I recorded the temperature of the air as it passed through the skirt of the SolPOD to see where the greatest heat loss was. Additionally I recorded the pre and post drying mass of the food that to determine the effectiveness of the drying ability of the SolPOD.

I found that there was a huge drop in tempeature as the air transfered from the skirt to the crown, most likely due to the drastic change in diameter. I I also found that when given eight hours or more the slices of food would lose around 30-50% of their mass. I concluded that although the SolPOD was able to succesfully dry food put into it, if some changes were made to the crown greater food drying could be acheived in less time.
